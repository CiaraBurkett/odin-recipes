Zucchini Hummus Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 large leaves romaine lettuce
  • ⅓ cup zucchini hummus
  • ¼ red bell pepper, sliced into rings
  • 2 tablespoons sliced black olives

Equipment

  • measuring cups
  • rubber spatula
  • cutting board
  • serrated knife, 5-inch
  • chef's knife, 8-inch

Instructions

Spread one of the lettuce leaves with the hummus and arrange the bell pepper and olives over it. Put the second lettuce leaf on top. Serve immediately.

Yield: 1 serving
