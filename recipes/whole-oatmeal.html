Whole Oatmeal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup soaked oat groats
  • ½ apple, peeled and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup or agave nectar
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ½ almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons raisins, soaked in water for ten minutes and drained, or 2 tablespoon dried compote

Equipment

  • measuring cups
  • cutting board
  • peeler
  • chef's knife, 8-inch
  • measuring spoons
  • food processor
  • rubber spatula
  • small bowl
  • small colander or fine-mesh strainer
  • small saucepan

Instructions

Put the oat groats, apple, water, maple syrup, cinnamon, optional vanilla extract, and salt in a food processor fitted with the S blade and process until smooth, stopping occasionally to scrape down the work bowl with a rubber spatula. Serve immediately with the almond milk and raisins.

Warming Option: Put in a small saucepan over low heat and warm gently for 2 to 3 minutes before adding the almond milk and raisins.

Yield: 1 cup, 2 servings
